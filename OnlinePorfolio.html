About Us

We are NordByte, a team of 1st year college students with a growing passion for technology and programming. Our interests are in solving issues, make new and innovative programs, and develop websites. We immerse ourselfs in hands on expirences by subjecting ourselves to challenges to be better because we are eager to learn and broaden our skill sets. Each member of this team brings unique perspective & thinking which helps us tackle challenges creatively and work more effectively. We aim to dedicate ourselves to learning from our mistakes and persist in order to pursue excellence of the highest measure we are able to produce. Through these projects we will be able build our skills, technical abilities as well as improve our ability in communication, teamwork, and coordination as future professionals in the field.
